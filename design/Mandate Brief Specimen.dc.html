Executive snapshot

·Revenue model is B2B distribution to licensed dealers; the company states 24,000 MT p.a. capacity at Nashik. 7
·Two production units (Nashik, Indore); ~380 employees per the company's careers page. 7,8
·Series B funded (2024); Elevar Agri Partners holds one board seat; promoters retain majority per press reporting. 10,11

·Most significant matter: a 2025 charge in favour of Axis Bank is registered against the Nashik plant; scope and outstanding amount need confirmation at kickoff. 5
1 · Business and operating footprint
The company manufactures organic and bio-fertilisers, micronutrient blends and soil conditioners, selling to licensed agri-input dealers rather than to farmers directly. 7 Its website lists production units at Nashik (Maharashtra) and Indore (Madhya Pradesh) and depots in four further states; the Indore unit does not appear in registry filings and may be leased — not publicly established. 7,4 Trade-mark records show "MERIDIAN AGRO" registered in classes 1 and 5 since 2021. 9
Information not publicly established: ownership vs lease of the Indore unit · dealer-network contracts · employee headcount beyond the company's own statement.
2 · Industry and competitive position
Indian bio-fertiliser manufacture is licence-driven (FCO) with state-level distribution controls; consolidation has drawn PE interest to mid-size producers. 12
