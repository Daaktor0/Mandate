# 16 — Open Decisions and Adopted Assumptions

## Finalised naming decision

- **Product:** Mandate.
- **Flagship report:** Mandate Brief.
- “Mandate Brief” is the generated user-facing output.
- Generic technical entities may retain names such as `reports`, `report_jobs` and `report_versions` where that improves code clarity.

## Founder decisions adopted

Separate product with possible Closing Room integration; partners/associates as day-to-day users; transaction-preparation positioning; Indian and cross-border context; Indian private, public unlisted and listed primary entities; website or legal name initially; mandatory entity confirmation; transaction type does not restrict base research; kickoff questions mandatory; public credible information only; social only when useful; latest three financial years for dynamic information; public-risk/litigation where reliable; sources in source section; neutral lawyer tone; “Matters for attention”; editable/versioned Mandate Briefs and letterhead; Google/Microsoft login; individual accounts; no public share link; failed generation does not consume value; first 100 eligible users receive one free Mandate Brief; regeneration is new; issue reporting; Hostinger/Supabase/OpenRouter and AWS portability; approximately ₹1,000 prototype incremental budget; async generation + email; admin dashboard; public-information/no-legal-advice disclosure; and no user/firm/private mandate data to OpenRouter.

## Defaults chosen

Transaction type optional after preliminary research; client role mandatory; primary entity India-incorporated with foreign counterparties contextual; default two pages and one-to-four-page range; suggestions/questions but no formal conclusions; one primary + up to two material related entities; Mandate Briefs until deletion with short-lived raw pages/letterhead; edit learning opt-in/anonymised/curated; pricing ₹999 single, ₹3,499/5 and ₹5,999/10; Supabase Queue + Python Hostinger worker; low concurrency and AWS interfaces.

## Deferred

Final visual design, source-annex default, saved letterhead, subscription, workspaces, enterprise SSO, foreign primary targets, paid MCA documents, legal-database vendor, premium human review, mobile app, Closing Room integration, exact small model/GPU, collaboration and group pricing.

## Empirical tests needed

Mandate Brief cost, search provider, value of paid company data, generation time, Hostinger capacity, willingness to pay, page preference, listed-company complexity, edit behaviour, source-annex preference, support/refunds and litigation-match quality.

## Founder checklist before final build

Confirm pack pricing/expiry, 24-hour letterhead deletion, India-incorporated target scope, up to two related entities, optional transaction type, mandatory client role, phone verification for trial and opt-in edit training.

The product naming decision is finalised and no longer open.
